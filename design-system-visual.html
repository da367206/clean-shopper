Brand Direction
Trustworthy, approachable, and grounded — a wellness tool that earns credibility through clarity rather than clinical severity. Draws from Thrive Market and Grove Collaborative: balanced layouts, natural color anchored in muted green, warm neutral surfaces that signal transparency without sterility. It should feel like a knowledgeable friend, not a laboratory report.
