## 1. Problem Statement
Consumers who want to avoid toxic, harmful, or environmentally damaging home and pantry products face an overwhelming research burden: ingredient lists require expertise to decode, "clean" and "natural" marketing claims are unreliable, and the information needed to make an informed decision is scattered across multiple sources with no persistent place to record what they've learned.

## 2. ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
A household purchaser — likely a parent or health-conscious adult — who has already decided they want to buy cleaner products but is stalled by the effort required to act on that intent. They currently read labels, search review sites, or ask friends, but find the process fragmented and slow. They have some awareness of certification labels (organic, EWG Verified) but lack the expertise to evaluate ingredient safety on their own. They shop primarily online or at grocery/retail stores and are not looking to change where they shop — just what they buy.

## 3. Pain Points
- Ingredient lists are technical and require specialist knowledge to interpret safely
- "Clean," "natural," and "non-toxic" labels on products are unverified marketing claims, not reliable signals
- Finding trustworthy alternatives requires cross-referencing multiple websites and databases — slow and fragmented
- Preferences and trusted knowledge built up over time (avoided ingredients, trusted brands) have no home and must be reconstructed each time

## 4. Proposed Solution
- Users can describe what product they're looking for in natural language and receive researched recommendations with ingredient-safety reasoning
- Users can ask Clean Shopper to evaluate a specific product against clean standards
- Users can save preferences — ingredients to avoid, trusted brands, certifications that matter — and have them applied automatically to future recommendations
- Users can add recommended products to a persistent shopping cart
- Users can ask Clean Shopper to compare two or more products and receive a clear recommendation based on their saved preferences
